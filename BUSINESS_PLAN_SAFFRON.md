# Business Plan: Kashmiri Saffron Direct Trade (India → EU)

## Executive Summary
Premium Grade-I Mongra saffron from Pampore, Kashmir, sold in small premium jars to European culinary/health market. Light, non-perishable, high value-density — ideal for Nostr/crypto distribution.

## Product
- Grade-I Mongra saffron (highest grade, all-red stigma)
- Source: Pampore cooperative, Kashmir
- GI-tagged "Kashmir Saffron" (EU recognizes this GI)
- Packaging: 1g, 2g, 5g premium glass jars with provenance card

## Financial Model
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Wholesale cost (1g Grade-I) | €2.50-4.00 |
| EU retail (1g premium jar) | €15-30 |
| EU retail (5g premium jar) | €60-100 |
| Gross margin | 400-700% |
| Net margin (after shipping/customs/packaging) | 60-75% |
| Target volume (month 1-3) | 50-100 units |
| Monthly revenue at 100 × 1g @ €20 | €2,000 |
| Monthly revenue at 50 × 5g @ €70 | €3,500 |
| Monthly gross profit | €1,200-2,600 |

## Legal Requirements
- [ ] Food spice classification (established in EU pre-1997, no Novel Food issue)
- [ ] EU pesticide residue limits (MRL) — test every batch (€80-150/test)
- [ ] Food hygiene registration (HACCP basics)
- [ ] Traceability documentation (batch tracking)
- [ ] Import declaration, 0% customs duty on saffron (HS 0910.20)
- [ ] 19% import VAT
- [ ] Gewerbeanmeldung, Impressum, Widerrufsrecht
- [ ] No health claims — sell as spice/food only

## Sourcing Strategy
- Contact Pampore saffron cooperatives directly
- Request GI certification with each batch
- Laboratory test for pesticide residues before EU import
- Start with 500g batch (enough for 500 × 1g jars)
- Estimated first order cost: €1,250-2,000 + shipping + testing

## Sales Channels
1. **Plebeian Market** — fixed-price and auction for rare batches
2. **Sovereign Optics shop** — fixed price premium jars
3. **Nostr foodie/chef community** — recipe content + saffron sales
4. **Bitcoin/Nostr gift market** — premium positioning

## Risk Assessment
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Pesticide residue failure | Medium | High | Lab test every batch before import |
| Adulteration (fake saffron) | Medium | Critical | GI certification + lab ID test |
| Low volume per sale | High | Low | Bundle 5g jars, gift sets |
| Competition from Iranian saffron | High | Medium | Kashmiri GI differentiation, story |
| Customs delay | Low | Low | Small parcels ship fast |

## Viability Score: 8/10
- Highest margin per gram of any product
- Very low shipping cost (light, small)
- Legal pathway clear (established food spice)
- Main constraint: volume (small product, need repeat customers)
