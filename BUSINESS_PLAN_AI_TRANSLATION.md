# Business Plan: AI-Assisted Translation Service (DE/EN/HI)

## Executive Summary
Translation service leveraging AI (DeepL/GPT) with human review for German↔English↔Hindi. Target: Berlin expats, Indian tech immigrants, visa/bureaucracy documents.

## Product
- Document translation: DE↔EN, EN↔HI, DE↔HI
- Specializations:
  - Official documents (birth certificates, marriage certs, transcripts)
  - Visa/immigration documents
  - Academic transcripts/credentials
  - Business correspondence
  - Technical documentation (leverages hardware/engineering background)
- AI-assisted first draft + human review/edit = fast turnaround, low cost
- Delivery: PDF + notarized option (partner with a certified translator for stamping)

## Financial Model
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| AI API cost (DeepL Pro) | €5.49/month (500k chars) or €25/month (unlimited) |
| Marginal cost per page | €0.10-0.50 (AI + electricity) |
| Price per page (standard) | €15-30 |
| Price per page (certified/notarized) | €35-60 |
| Target: 50 pages/month | €750-1,500 revenue |
| Target: 150 pages/month | €2,250-4,500 revenue |
| Gross margin | 80% |
| Net margin (after platform fees, marketing) | 60-70% |

## Competitive Advantage
- India connection = native Hindi + cultural context
- Berlin location = understands German bureaucracy
- Hardware/engineering background = technical translation expertise
- Nostr/Bitcoin payment option (unique for immigrants without EU bank)
- Fast turnaround (AI draft + review = 2-4 hours vs 2-3 days human-only)

## Sales Channels
1. Plebeian Market / Sovereign Optics (Nostr-native clients)
2. Berlin expat Facebook/Telegram groups
3. Kleinanzeigen (German classifieds)
4. Fiverr/Upwork (for initial pipeline)
5. Partnerships with relocation services

## Risk Assessment
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Google Translate commoditizes | High | Medium | Specialize in certified/technical translation |
| Certified translator requirement | Medium | Medium | Partner with a sworn translator for stamping |
| Quality errors | Medium | High | Human review every document, liability disclaimer |
| Low volume initially | High | Medium | Multiple channels, competitive pricing |
| Competition from established agencies | High | Medium | Niche focus (Hindi + tech + Bitcoin payment) |

## Viability Score: 8/10
- Fastest to launch (days, not weeks)
- Lowest startup cost (just API subscription)
- Immediate demand in Berlin (thousands of Indian tech immigrants)
- Main risk: commoditization by free tools — mitigate with specialization
