# Business Plan: Pashmina Direct Trade (India → EU)

## Executive Summary
Direct-trade authentic Kashmiri Pashmina shawls/scarves sourced from GI-certified producers in Kashmir, sold to European boutique/Bitcoin/Nostr audience via Plebeian Market (auctions) and Sovereign Optics web shop (fixed price).

## Product
- 100% genuine hand-spun, hand-woven pashmina (cashmere goat undercoat)
- Source: GI-certified producers in Srinagar, Kashmir
- Variants: Plain pashmina, Kani embroidery, Sozni hand-embroidered
- Unique selling point: Provenance-verified, direct-from-source, blockchain/Nostr attestation possible

## Legal Requirements
- [ ] Textile labeling compliance (EU 1007/2011) — fiber composition label
- [ ] REACH compliance — azo-dye test (€50/test per batch)
- [ ] Import declaration + 12% customs duty (HS code 6214)
- [ ] 19% Einfuhrumsatzsteuer (import VAT)
- [ ] Gewerbeanmeldung (trade registration)
- [ ] Impressum on shop (fines up to €50k for non-compliance)
- [ ] 14-day Widerrufsrecht (return right)
- [ ] Product liability insurance (~€200-400/year)
- [ ] NEVER source Shahtoosh (CITES-banned, criminal offense)

## Sourcing Strategy
- Contact GI-certified producers: Kashmir Loom Co., Heritage Pashmina
- Verify authenticity: burn test + microscope check
- Start with small batch (20 units) to test quality and demand
- Build direct relationship with one cooperative for consistent supply
- Negotiate exclusivity for EU market if volume justifies

## Sales Channels
1. **Plebeian Market (primary)** — auction format for unique embroidered pieces
2. **Sovereign Optics shop** — fixed-price for standard pashmina
3. **Nostr-native marketing** — provenance story, photos, artisan profiles
4. **Bitcoin conference markets** — physical pop-up at Bitcoin events

## Risk Assessment
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Counterfeit pashmina from supplier | Medium | High | Burn test + microscope, GI certification |
| Customs delay/seizure | Low | Medium | Proper labeling, commercial invoice, phytosanitary cert |
| Azo-dye REACH failure | Low | High | Lab test every batch before shipping |
| Low demand | Low | High | Start with 20-unit batch, test before scaling |
| Supplier reliability | Medium | Medium | Multiple supplier contacts, escrow/payment terms |

## Viability Score: 8/10
- High margin, proven demand, legal pathway clear
- Main risk: quality control and supplier authenticity
- Best single-product category by revenue potential
